Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Welcome to Rasoi Plan (also referred to as "Meal Plan"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account and use the App:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and password.
  • Profile Data: Your dietary preferences (e.g., Jain, Pure Veg), region, and profile picture (avatar).
  • Device Permissions: With your permission, we may access your device's Camera and Photo Library to allow you to upload a profile picture. We only access images you specifically choose to upload.
  • App Activity: Information about the meals you 'Favorite', 'Dislike', or 'Swap' to help generate better weekly plans for you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's core functionality (generating custom weekly meal plans).
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Customize your experience based on your dietary preferences.
  • Communicate with you, including sending password reset emails or important account updates.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services like Supabase for secure database and authentication management. These services are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with a legal process or government request.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. Your passwords are securely encrypted by our authentication provider (Supabase).

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the App settings.
  • Camera/Photo Permissions: You can revoke the App's access to your camera and photo library at any time through your device's OS settings. Doing so will only prevent you from updating your profile picture.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@rasoiplan.com.
