Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Rasoi Plan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Rasoi Plan app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to provide and improve the app.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address (if you sign up or log in).
  • Preferences: Dietary style (Vegetarian, Jain, Lacto‑Ovo), dislikes, spice level, and meal preferences.
  • Optional Inputs: Household size, cooking time preference, and other non‑medical lifestyle choices.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version.
  • Usage Data: Screens viewed, meals saved, interactions within the app.
  • Analytics Data: Through trusted third‑party tools (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics).

1.3 What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Medical or health data
  • Diagnoses, conditions, symptoms
  • Weight, labs, vitals
  • Protected health information (PHI)
  • Sensitive personal identifiers

Rasoi Plan is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Personalize meal recommendations
  • Generate weekly meal plans
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Maintain and secure your account
  • Send optional notifications (e.g., reminders, updates)

We do not use your information for:

  • Medical purposes
  • Health analysis
  • Advertising targeting
  • Selling data to third parties

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data only with:

  • Service Providers who help operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics, authentication)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

All service providers are required to protect your information and use it only for app‑related functions.

We do not share your data with:

  • Advertisers
  • Employers
  • Insurance companies
  • Third‑party marketers

4. Data Storage & Security

We use industry‑standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure authentication
  • Restricted access controls

While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to safeguard your data.

5. Your Choices & Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your information
  • Update your account details
  • Delete your account
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of notifications

6. Children's Privacy

Rasoi Plan is not intended for children under 13. Users under 18 should use the app with parental guidance.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. International Users

If you access the app from outside the United States, you agree that your information may be processed and stored in the United States.

8. Changes to This Policy
